FAQ

A few things people usually want to know before reaching out

Will this actually sound like me?＋
That’s the whole point. My job isn’t to paste a style onto you. It’s to understand how you think, what you notice, what you care about, and how you naturally communicate, then shape that into a stronger online presence.
I don’t want to overshare online. Is that a problem?＋
No. This isn’t about turning LinkedIn into a diary. We use stories in a controlled, useful way, with enough humanity to build trust without making your content feel exposed or unprofessional.
What if I don’t have time for this?＋
That’s exactly why people hire me. The process is designed to pull strong material from conversations, voice notes, and the things you’re already doing, then turn that into assets that support your business.
Why not just use AI?＋
AI can help with speed, but it can’t supply lived perspective. It doesn’t know your standards, your trade-offs, or where your credibility actually comes from. People can feel the difference.
Do you only do done-for-you ghostwriting?＋
No. I also offer strategic support and consulting for people who want help with positioning, profile improvement, content direction, or storytelling without handing everything over.
Who do you usually work with?＋
Usually founders, CEOs, and established B2B leaders whose expertise is real, but whose LinkedIn presence is either underdeveloped, too generic, or not yet doing justice to the level they operate at.
